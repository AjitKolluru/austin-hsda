About Us

What We Do

HSDA's mission is to engage, mobilize, and amplify high school students in Democratic political organizing and activism. We provide a platform for young voices to make a difference in their communities and beyond.

What We Believe

Our organization stands for equity, climate justice, labor rights, and progressive principles. We believe in creating a more just and inclusive society through democratic participation and advocacy.

Who We Are

We are part of a national organization with local chapters across the country. Our Austin chapter is led by dedicated student officers and supported by passionate members committed to democratic values.

Our Core Values

  • Equity and Social Justice
  • Climate Action and Environmental Protection
  • Workers' Rights and Fair Labor Practices
  • Inclusive Democracy and Voting Rights
  • Educational Access and Student Rights
  • Community Engagement and Civic Participation
